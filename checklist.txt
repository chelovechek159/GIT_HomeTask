Checklist items for website functional testing:

-check the opening and availability of the site;

-check the opening and availability of the site again after it is closed;

-check that all menu items work;

-check that the buttons on the site are pressed;

-check that all links open;

-check, are broken links there;

-fill out and check all forms on the site;

-check the functionality of the main elements of the site;

-upload documents and check the correctness of the upload;

-and others.
